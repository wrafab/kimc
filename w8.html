Week 8 | MINI CAD


After an intense week of finishing my Compass project, I wanted to make a mini-furniture set for my cousin’s hamsters. Therefore, I downloaded files from opendesk cc furniture and changed the proportion. I printed out a small table and several stools. I really enjoy assembling these furnitures, so I hope to print out my uniquely designed furniture next year.



Process

1. Download/or create your own furniture layout with coreldraw. 2. Print it out! 3. Assemble the pieces




Outcome


I don’t know if the hamsters would actually be able to use them, but I’m looking forward to seeing them get to use it! (They used a mini swimming pool my cousin and I created for them)
