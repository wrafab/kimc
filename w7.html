Week 7 | EGGBOT


The task for this week was to create EGGBOT! The Eggbot is an art robot that can draw on spherical or egg-shaped products. The size of the sphere can vary from a ping pong all to a small grapefruit. (1.25~4.25 inches in diameter). The kit is based on the original design by Bruce Shapiro. We created two robots: 1) EggBot 2) B-Robot



Process

1. Print all the required pieces using the makerbot. 2. Assemble all the pieces (the robot and the eggbot) 3. Download Inkscape on a computer and an "eggbot" extension. 4. Set up the eggbot (set up the height of the pen and screw the sides in) 5. Connect the eggbot to the computer. 6. Once you click send, all the information will be sent to the eggbot.
